Dualist - Simple, Adaptive, Private.

Dualist is a streamlined, high-performance To-Do List application designed for the modern Android ecosystem. It focuses on a seamless transition between mobile and large-screen form factors using a state-of-the-art adaptive architecture.

🌟 KEY FEATURES:

🌓 ADAPTIVE 2-PANE LAYOUT
- Optimized List-Detail pattern that automatically expands to two panes on tablets and foldables.
- Providing a high-productivity workspace for large-screen devices.

🏠 OFFLINE-FIRST & PRIVATE
- All your tasks are stored locally on your device in a robust Room database.
- No cloud sync, no data mining, and no accounts required. Your data stays with you.

🚫 NO GOOGLE SERVICES REQUIRED
- Completely free of Google Play Services (GMS).
- Runs perfectly on de-googled Android distributions and privacy-focused ROMs.

🎨 VIBRANT MATERIAL 3 DESIGN
- A bold, energetic color scheme utilizing the latest Material Design 3 principles.
- Full support for Light and Dark modes.
- Modern edge-to-edge display integration.

✅ CORE TASK MANAGEMENT
- Effortlessly add, check off, and delete tasks.
- Intuitive and responsive interface designed for speed and clarity.

Dualist is the perfect companion for anyone seeking a fast, private, and beautifully designed task manager that feels at home on any device size. Download Dualist today and experience a new standard in adaptive productivity!
