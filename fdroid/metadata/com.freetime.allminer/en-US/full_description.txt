AllMiner is a powerful crypto miner optimized specifically for Android devices. It utilizes the original RandomX engine to enable efficient hashing directly on your smartphone's CPU.

Features:
* Native RandomX Engine: Optimized for ARM64 processors.
* unMineable Support: Mine coins like DOGE, SHIB, SOL, ADA, and many more directly to your wallet.
* Multi-Threading: Choose the number of CPU cores for maximum performance or minimum heat generation.
* Hardware Protection: Automatic stop on overheating or low battery (< 20%).
* Transparent & Open-Source: No hidden fees, full control over your hardware.

Note: Mining on a smartphone can strain the hardware. Use the integrated heat protection for safe operation.
